A Bespoke Sales Process, Curated for You.

We don't run a clock. We run a confidential matching process designed for high-value assets. Here is how we secure your outcome:

  1. Confidential Consultation

    Our specialists contact you for a private, no-obligation discussion. We verify your vehicle’s provenance, history, and condition to craft a tailored sales strategy.

  2. Curated Presentation

    A professional photoshoot and a bespoke digital brochure are created. Your asset is presented exclusively to our pre-vetted network of global collectors—never in a public “lot.”

  3. Discreet Negotiation

    We manage all inquiries and negotiate directly with qualified buyers on your behalf. Your identity and your asset’s details remain confidential until terms are agreed.

  4. Seamless Settlement

    We oversee all escrow arrangements, legal paperwork, and logistics. You receive secure payment upon successful transfer of ownership.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a private sales treaty, and how is it different from an auction?

Unlike an auction with a fixed end date, a private treaty allows us to negotiate directly with qualified buyers on your behalf, often achieving a higher price without time pressure. Your asset remains off public marketplaces.

What fees and commissions do you charge?

Our fee structure is performance-based and fully transparent. A success fee applies only upon a completed sale. No hidden listing fees.

How is my privacy protected?

Your personal information and the vehicle’s location are only shared with serious, pre-qualified buyers after a signed NDA. All marketing is generic or anonymized unless you approve otherwise.

Not sure if your vehicle qualifies?

Contact our specialists for a confidential pre-assessment. Contact us — no obligation, fully discreet.
