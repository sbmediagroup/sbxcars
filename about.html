About us

SBX Cars is owned and operated by Supercar Blondie (SB). We are the only digital platform specializing in confidential, white-glove sales of rare and collectable vehicles from all over the world.

The SBX team has deep expertise in both traditional auction houses and digital marketplaces, and is based in Europe, America, and the Middle East.

WHY SBX CARS?

SBX Cars is not an auction. It is a private sales treaty platform built on trust, discretion, and the global standard of Supercar Blondie.

Global Reach

Through the Supercar Blondie brand, SBX Cars has unrivalled access to 120 million automotive enthusiasts. We find the right buyer, not just any buyer.

Curated Acceptance

SBX Cars focuses exclusively on premium vehicles. We do not list every car. Only those that meet our strict criteria for rarity, provenance, and condition.

Private & Global

We bring together sellers and qualified buyers from over 80 countries — without public auctions. Your vehicle is marketed discreetly to a hand-picked network.

White-Glove Service

The SBX team has years of experience in collector car transactions. From consultation to settlement, we handle every step with discretion and care.

Performance-Based

Our fee structure is transparent and success-based. No hidden listing fees. You only pay when your vehicle is sold.

Tailored Timeline

We do not rush. High-value assets require the right timing. We work on a schedule that suits you and attracts the right buyer.
